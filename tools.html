TOOLS FOR ELECTRICALS

Equipment

We have soo many hand-electricals tools to make working easier. So we will look at examples of the hand tools and their uses

STRAIGHT BLADE SCREWDRIVER

Screw driver is a hand tool that is used for turning screws. It consist of a metal rod with a flat or cross-shaped and that fit into the top of the screw. For screws with one straight diametral slot cut across the head, standards screwdrivers with flat blade tips and in a variety of sizes are used. A typical simple screwdriver has a handle and a shaft, ending in a tip the user put into the screw head before turning the handle.

UTILITY KNIFE

Utility knife is any type of knife used for general manual work purposes. Today the term utility knife also includes small folding, retractable- and/ or replaceable-razor blade knifes suited for use in the general work place or in the construction industries. It is a fantastic tool to have in the home tool box or junk drawer. It cut with a removable razor-blade, so the edge is both incredibly sharp and very disposable.

TONGUE AND GROOVE-PLIERS

Tongue and groove pliers are types of slip-joint pliers.They are commonly used for turning and holding nuts and bolts, gripping irregularly shaped objects,and clamping materials. It is also possible to rotate objects while keeping them in one position.

PHILIPS SCREWDRIVER

These tools are designed to fit screws with Phillips heads,which have a cross -shaped recess-hence the alternative name of cross screwdriver. They were invented to cope with higher torsion. A typical simple screwdriver has a handle and a shaft, ending in a tip the user puts into the screw head before turning the handle.

MULTIMETER

A multimeter is mainly used to measure the three basic electricals characteristics of voltage, current,and resistance. It can also be used to test continuity between two points in an electrical circuit.

TWIST DRILL

Twist drill bits are used for drilling through anything from wood to plastic to metal products,but not masonry and concrete products. However, their primary use is for drilling through metal.

SPIRIT LEVEL

A spirit level is used for determinind horizontal and vertical levels and can be used on a variety of DIY projects in and around the home. It is also used to designed,to indicate whether a surface is level on the horizontal or vertical planes,and there are a variety of sizes and designs for every project.

WIRE STRIPPERS

A wire stripper is a small hand-held tool that is used to remove the insulation from electric wires.It is portable and extremely popular among electricians and even DIYers for removing the insulation in order to replace or repair the wire.

NON-CONTACT VOLTAGE TESTER

A Non Contact Voltage Tester alllows you to check for the voltage in wires or devices without you having to touch any parts/wires

NEEDLE-NOSE PLIER

A Needle Nose Plier are both cutting and holding tool commonly used by artisian,electricians,engineers,jewellery designers and other tradesmen to bend,coil,re-position and snip wires. With the help of the long nose gives excellent control while the cutting edge near the pliers joint provides "one tool"convenience.

SCRIBER

A scriber is use for marking liner on work surfaces. It has a harden needle point which can be drawn along the straight edge of a rule or try square.

MICROMETER

It is use to take more exact measurement down to 0.01mm.It is an instrument of extreeme accuracy and must be treated with special care and kept in a nice case when not in use.

SAFETY AND WORKSHOP

Introduction

Employers and employees have a duty to perform that the place in which they work is free from danger which are likely to affect the health and safety of individuals and others.

SAFE WORKING PRACTICES

Under the health and safety at work ACT 1974,the employer must,

1. Ensure the health,safety and welfare of his employees.

2.Provide and maintain safe equipment.

3.Ensure the safety of employees in the use,handling,storage and transport of materials

EMPLOYEES DUTIES

The employee must;

1. Take care not to endange the health and safety of himself or others at work.

2.Co-operate with his employer in carrying out the duties previously mentioned and to report any danger of which his employer may be onaware.

3. Do not interfere with anything that has been provided to ensure the well-being of himself and others

Wiring Systems
Conduit wiring

A conduit wiring is an enclosure in which cables are drawn so that they are afforded mechanical protection. There various types of conduit some are rigid others are flexible some too are metalic others are non-metalic.

Types of Metal Conduit
1.Light guage steel conduit
2.Heavy gauge steel conduit
3.Copper gauge steel conduit
4.Flexible conduit
5.Aluminium conduit
Light Gauge Steel Conduit

They are conduit coated with zinc. In situations which where there is likelihood of greater mechanical damage to the wiring such as individual and commercial installation, steel conduit provides a suitable method of protection. The use of fittings such as bends etc; provides a means of joining the conduits in the various required directions.

Detailed Drawing

Is an additional drawing produced by the architect to clarify some pionts of detail. Thus a drawing might be produced to give fuller description of a particular electrical arrangement

Layout Drawing

Is a skilled drawing based upon the architect site plan of the drawing and shows the position of electrical equipment which is to installed. The electrical equipment is identified by a graphical symbol

Block Diagram

Is a very simple diagram in which the varios items or pieces of equipment are represented by a square or a rectangular box. The purpose of the block diagram is show how the component of the circuit relate to each other and therefore the individual circuit connections are not shown.

Wiring Diagram

Is a diagram which shows the detail between components or items of an equipment. They don't indicate how a piece of an equipment or circuits works. The purpose of the wiring diagram is to help someone with the actual wiring of the circuit.

Schematic Diagram

Is an outline diagram which uses graphical symbol to indicate the inter-relationship of the electrical symbol element in a circuit (eg:motor starter circuit). The diagram helps us to understand the working operation of a circuit and looks very much like a circuit diagram.
